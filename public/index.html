Brand override notes

These notes override every other rule the AI follows. Use them to correct anything it got wrong about your brand, add rules that aren't in your docs, or fix mistakes you keep seeing in generated captions.

How to use this
  • Write in plain English. No special format needed.
  • Be specific. "Don't use exclamation marks" beats "tone down energy."
  • Use examples. "Say 'guests' not 'customers'" is clearer than "fix the language."
  • Saves automatically apply to every future generation. No need to regenerate the Brain.
